Soup: Seafood Congee

A seafood congee is the rice soup consisting of shrimp, rice, green onions, and salt. It is unbelivably delicious and easy to cook.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Shrimp
  • Green onion
  • Salt

Directions:

  1. Cook a regular rice soup.
  2. When the rice soup is cooked, add shrimp to the mixture.
  3. Cook the soup until the shrimp is cooked.

  5. Add your liking amount of salt.
  6. Add the green onion and stir the soup a few times.
  7. The delicious seafood congee is ready to serve!
